About StationeryHub

StationeryHub is a web-based inventory management system designed to help offices and shops manage their stationery products efficiently.

Built using Jakarta EE 8, JavaServer Faces, MySQL 8.0 and Payara 5 as part of CS5003 coursework at London Metropolitan University.

Features: full CRUD operations, live Ajax search, category filtering, user authentication, session tracking and form validation.
